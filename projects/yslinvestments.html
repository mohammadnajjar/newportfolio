YSL Investments

Methods & journey

YSL Investments is the holding company behind multiple subsidiaries spanning real estate, technology, biofuels, and Islamic finance. They needed a single digital home that could represent the group professionally, showcase each brand, and serve different audiences — investors, press, and potential partners — without feeling generic.

The challenge

Representing a multi-brand holding group without losing brand coherence. Each subsidiary has its own identity, but they all need to feel part of a unified group. The website had to work equally well for a potential investor doing due diligence and a journalist looking for press assets.

  • Group overview and leadership section with professional bios
  • Individual subsidiary pages with branding, description, and live links
  • Investor relations: group overview, portfolio highlights, contact flow
  • Press center: news archive, downloadable press kits, media assets
  • CMS for the communications team to publish news without developer help
  • Bilingual support (Arabic / English) throughout

The architecture

Laravel with a custom CMS built on Filament — giving the communications team a clean, modern admin panel to manage news posts, press kits, and subsidiary information without touching code. Blade templates for server-side rendering ensure fast page loads and strong SEO for a corporate domain.

Bilingual content is handled through a locale-based content model, where each page, section, and media item has both Arabic and English variants managed through the same CMS interface.

What shipped

A live yslinvestments.com with full group and subsidiary pages, an investor relations section, a functioning press center, and a Filament CMS that the internal team uses independently to publish content.

The outcome

The comms team went from emailing me every time they needed a news post to publishing independently through the CMS. The website now serves as a credible first impression for investors and partners approaching the group.

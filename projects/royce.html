Royce

Royce

Methods & journey

Dubai's premium property buyers are impatient and well-informed. They compare three listings in a tab, they want the floor plan in one click, and they will not wait 4 seconds for filters to reload. Royce needed a platform that felt like an expensive product — fast, precise, and never in the way.

The challenge

1,800+ active listings across residential, commercial, and off-plan segments — each with its own schema, pricing model, and display needs. A content team that includes zero developers but updates 40+ listings every week. And a CRM that sales agents live inside, which had to exchange leads in real time without double-entry.

  • Sub-300ms filter response with 1,800+ listings under load
  • A CMS the content team can run without developer help
  • Two-way CRM sync (Salesforce) for every lead captured
  • Arabic/English bilingual with proper RTL everywhere
  • SEO-optimised listing URLs + rich snippets for Google real estate

The architecture

Laravel backend with Meilisearch powering the filters (search is the real bottleneck in real estate), React on the public site, and a custom admin panel on Inertia.js so the content team gets SPA speed without a second codebase to maintain. Salesforce sync runs on a dedicated queue with idempotent retries.

What shipped

Five months, including three weeks of content migration from the legacy system. The public site scored 94 on Lighthouse performance at launch and has stayed above 90 since. Content team now publishes in minutes, not the 45 minutes the old workflow required.

The outcome

Lead volume grew 2.3× in the first quarter against the previous site, driven primarily by better SEO on off-plan listings and a property- detail page that converts at 3× the old rate.

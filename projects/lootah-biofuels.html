Lootah Biofuels

Methods & journey

Lootah Biofuels is one of the UAE's leading sustainable energy companies, converting used cooking oil into biodiesel for the transport sector. They needed two things: a credible corporate web presence and a mobile app to serve their operations and stakeholders. I delivered both.

The challenge

The corporate website needed to communicate sustainability credentials and technical expertise to a professional B2B audience — regulators, fuel buyers, and environmental partners. The mobile app backend needed to handle operations data, reports, and user management for field and office staff.

  • Corporate website: sustainability story, product catalog, certifications, contact
  • News & press section with CMS for internal team
  • App backend: user auth, role management, operations data, reporting API
  • Admin panel for managing app users, reports, and content
  • SEO-optimized for biofuel industry keywords in the UAE
  • Performance: all pages under 2s on mobile (Lighthouse 90+)

The architecture

The corporate website runs on Laravel with Blade templates — static-first for fast delivery and SEO, with a Filament CMS for the news section. The mobile app backend shares the same Laravel installation via a versioned API namespace, keeping infrastructure lean and deployment simple.

The mobile app API handles authentication, user role assignment (admin, field operator, auditor), operations log submission, and a reporting endpoint that aggregates collection and production data for management views.

What shipped

A live lootahbiofuels.com with all corporate sections and a functioning CMS. A production API powering the iOS and Android apps. Admin panel for user and content management. All delivered within the project timeline.

The outcome

The website became the authoritative digital presence for the brand — ranking for relevant sustainability and biofuel keywords in the UAE. The app gave the field and management teams a shared platform for tracking operations in real time.

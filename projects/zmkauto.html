ZMK Auto

Methods & journey

ZMK Auto needed to digitise their entire customer journey — from browsing inventory online, to submitting a trade-in, to being matched with the right sales person. Their existing process was phone calls and spreadsheets. I built the full platform from scratch, solo, and handed over a live production website with a complete dealer back-office.

The challenge

Automotive inventory is complex — every vehicle has dozens of spec attributes, multiple images, condition grades, pricing tiers, and availability status. On top of that, the trade-in flow needed to capture vehicle condition accurately from the customer side, and route that lead to the right team member based on brand, location, and workload.

  • Searchable vehicle catalog with 30+ filterable spec attributes
  • Multi-image upload per listing with ordering and thumbnails
  • Trade-in value estimator with condition questionnaire
  • Lead routing engine: assign by brand specialist and current workload
  • Sales team dashboard: pipeline, follow-ups, and conversion tracking
  • Management reporting: inventory turnover, lead-to-sale rate, monthly targets

The architecture

Laravel handles the backend with a structured vehicle data model supporting arbitrary spec attributes (make, model, year, trim, color, mileage, condition, and dozens more) through a flexible EAV-inspired approach that avoids schema changes when new attributes are added. Vue.js powers the dealer admin for fast reactive interactions — adding listings, managing leads, updating status without page reloads.

The public-facing catalog uses Laravel Blade with server-side rendering for SEO performance. Vehicle search is handled with database full-text + indexed filter columns, keeping p95 search responses under 200ms.

What shipped

A fully live zmkauto.com with browsable vehicle inventory, trade-in submission form, lead capture, and a complete dealer back-office with reporting dashboards. Everything built and deployed solo.

The outcome

The sales team moved all lead management off WhatsApp and into the platform. Management got real-time visibility into inventory status and sales pipeline for the first time. Trade-in submissions captured through the website replaced walk-in-only enquiries.

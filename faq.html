Working together

Availability, scope, collaboration model.

What's your current availability?
+
I accept a maximum of 3 concurrent projects to protect quality. Typical project start is 1–2 weeks after contract signing. For urgent needs I offer a rush option with a 20% premium.
Do you work with international clients?
+
Yes. I'm based in Dubai but most of my freelance work is remote. I regularly collaborate with clients across Europe, the US, and MENA. Daily standup in your timezone is not a problem.
Do you work alone or with a team?
+
For small-to-medium projects (under $10k): solo. For larger enterprise engagements I lead a hand-picked team of 2–5 trusted engineers under my direct supervision. You always deal with me as the primary contact.
What if I only have a rough idea, not a spec?
+
That's the norm, not the exception. The discovery call and proposal stage exist exactly for that — we turn your rough idea into a scoped, estimated, realistic plan. No need to write a spec to get started.
Can you take over a project someone else started?
+
Yes, but I require a paid technical audit first ($1,200, fixed). This protects us both — I can see what I'm inheriting, and you get a written report either way, even if we decide not to continue together.

Pricing & payment

How engagements are priced and how money moves.

What's your payment structure?
+
Standard: 30% upfront, 40% at mid-project milestone, 30% upon delivery. Accepted methods: bank transfer (AED, USD, EUR), Wise, PayPal, or crypto (USDT). All fees are fixed — no hidden surprises.
Do you offer hourly rates?
+
For ad-hoc work, yes — $75/hour. For full projects, I strongly prefer fixed pricing. Fixed pricing aligns incentives: I'm paid to deliver outcomes, not to spend hours.
What happens if scope changes mid-project?
+
Change requests get a written mini-proposal: what it is, how it impacts timeline, and a fixed price add-on. You approve or decline before I touch a line of code. No silent scope creep.
Do you invoice in AED, USD, or EUR?
+
All three. Invoices are issued from my Dubai entity with proper VAT handling. I can also invoice in GBP on request.
Are there any discounts?
+
Nonprofits and early-stage founders (pre-seed, no funding) can ask for a 15% scope discount. I don't negotiate pricing otherwise — my rates are already set fairly for the deliverable.

Delivery & support

What you get, and what happens after launch.

What if I need changes after delivery?
+
Every project comes with 30 days of free bug fixes. For new features or major changes, monthly maintenance retainers start at $800/month. Hourly rate $75/hour for ad-hoc work.
Where does the code get deployed?
+
On your infrastructure, always. Your cloud, your servers, your domain. I don't host client code. You own the deployment pipeline and credentials from day one.
What if you become unavailable mid-project?
+
Every project has a written continuity plan — repository access, deployment runbook, and architecture doc. If something happens to me, any competent Laravel/React engineer can pick up where I left off. No lock-in.
Do you provide documentation?
+
Yes — architecture overview, API reference (OpenAPI spec), a deployment runbook, and a short handover video. All included. Documentation is a deliverable, not an afterthought.
How fast do you respond during a project?
+
Average response time during active work: under 4 hours in business hours (GST/GMT+4). For urgent production issues on clients with retainers, under 1 hour.

Legal & IP

Who owns what, and how we protect it.

How do you handle intellectual property?
+
I sign NDAs before any detailed discussion — free of charge. All code, files, and documentation belong to you 100%. I don't use your project in my portfolio without explicit written permission.
Do you use open-source or proprietary libraries?
+
I use permissively-licensed open-source libraries (MIT, Apache, BSD). No AGPL, no copyleft surprises, no commercial SDKs without your explicit approval. Every dependency is audited before shipping.
What contract do you work under?
+
Either my standard MSA + SOW, or yours — I'm happy to review and sign reasonable client agreements. Dubai jurisdiction by default, but I've worked under English, EU and US law as well.
Can I see examples of past NDAs or contracts?
+
Templates yes, signed ones no — that would defeat the point of the NDA. Ask and I'll send you my current MSA and mutual NDA templates for your legal team to review.
Do you carry professional insurance?
+
Yes — professional indemnity coverage via a Dubai-based insurer. Certificate of coverage available on request during contracting.
